CardioMetrix

CardioMetrix is being developed as an intelligent system for tracking and analyzing chronic indicators of cardiometabolic disorders, including obesity, diabetes, and hypertension. It is powered by an advanced AI model named MIKASA, designed to analyze patient data, detect risk patterns, alert users and caregivers, and provide personalized support.
